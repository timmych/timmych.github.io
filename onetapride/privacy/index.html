Privacy Policy

OneTapRide

OneTapRide does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app provides a simplified interface that launches Lyft or Uber using preset destinations. All ride handling and payment is managed through the Lyft or Uber apps, which are subject to their own privacy policies.

The app does not require user sign-in and does not track location or usage outside the functions provided by iOS and the installed ride-hailing apps.

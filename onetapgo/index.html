Welcome to OneTapGo

OneTapGo is designed specifically to help seniors and their caregivers stay organized when going places. The app helps you remember important items to bring and provides easy access to transportation options for your saved locations.

Getting Started

Explore the Places tab
View your saved locations and tap any place to see transportation options and reminders.
Check your Items
Browse your important items like medications, emergency contacts, and insurance information.
Ask your caregiver to customize
Have your caregiver use the Settings tab to add new places and items specific to your needs.

Using Places

  • View Places: Tap any place card to see transportation options
  • Get Directions: Choose from driving directions and public transit
  • See Reminders: Each place shows what items to remember to bring
  • Ride Services: Transportation options depend on what apps you have installed

  • Photos: Save pictures of important documents or items
  • Quick Access: Tap any item to view its full contents

Settings & Customization

Note: Settings are typically managed by caregivers or family members.

  • Manage Places: Add, edit, or remove saved locations
  • Manage Items: Create and organize notes, lists, and photo collections
  • Customize Appearance: Add emojis and colors to make items easier to identify
  • Reorder Items: Drag to rearrange places and items by importance

Your Privacy is Protected

100% Local Storage

OneTapGo respects your privacy and keeps your information completely secure:

  • No Data Collection: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information
  • Local Storage Only: All your places, items, and notes are stored only on your device
  • No Internet Required: The app works completely offline (except for transportation services)
  • No Tracking: We don't track your usage, location, or behavior
  • No Accounts: No sign-up required, no passwords, no user accounts
  • No Sharing: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it

Transportation Services: When you choose to use Uber, Lyft, or Apple Maps, those services operate under their own privacy policies. OneTapGo simply opens those apps - we don't share your data with them.

Your information belongs to you and stays with you.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Use large, clear emojis to make places and items easier to identify
  • Keep item names simple and descriptive
  • Regularly review and update your information with your caregiver
  • Take photos of important documents to have them readily available
  • Make sure your caregiver's phone number is up-to-date in the "My Caregiver" item
  • Enable location services for accurate pickup location sharing
  • If you prefer ride-sharing apps, ask your caregiver to install Uber or Lyft for direct booking

Need Help?

OneTapGo is designed to be simple and intuitive. If you need assistance:

  • Ask your caregiver or family member to help with setup
  • The app includes example items to help you get started
  • All information is stored safely on your device - you can't lose it
